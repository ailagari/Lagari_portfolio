My Story

A little bit about myself

It is my immense pleasure to introduce myself. I am Lagari.A. I come from the southern part of IndiaIndia. We are a middleclass family consisting of my father mother and two younger brothers. My father is retired as Polytechnic Principal of a reputed state funded college. We are a close knit happy bunch, well settled in the village area of Kerala.From my childhood days I was really fascinated and interested in arts and science. It may sound foolish of me but I often ponder into scientific questions for long periods of time lost in thought.

This inquisitive nature demanded lots of reading often probing into unknown subjects, in the process helping me gain a broader appreciation and understanding of things.I spend my free time to help people in my village especially students for doing science projects and other artistic works. I simply love to do crazy and interesting things! Interestingly the craziness mostly ended in short circuits in my room, fire blasts etc. but I keep experimenting. It’s not every day that you have a very good supporting family! During my senior years after the 10th grade I won prizes for district science fairs and I helped prepare other students for the same. My physics teachers inspired me a lot. During my spare time I also wrote and published research papers in a wide variety of topics ranging from physics to psychology. All my papers are the results of my continuous search for the deeper understanding of nature’s delights and mysteries. Completed my Bachelors in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, I am currently working as Mechatronics Engineer in UAE and also working to improvise my work on artificial intelligence with self-generative neural networks.
